System Health

Monitor your system’s health in real-time with detailed insights into CPU, RAM, disk usage, and running processes. Additionally, stay informed and troubleshoot instantly with an interactive tech support chatbot.

  • Display live data on CPU, RAM, and disk usage with easy-to-read charts and progress bars.

Process Manager

  • Show currently running processes with options to filter, sort, and terminate them if necessary.

Tech Support Chatbot

  • Integrate an AI-powered chatbot for troubleshooting common system issues.
  • Offers step-by-step guidance, FAQs, and direct links to helpful resources.
