Tortellini Recipe

Description

Tortellini is a type of stuffed pasta typical of the Italian cities of Bologna and Modena. Traditionally it is stuffed with a mix of meat (pork loin, raw prosciutto, mortadella), Parmesan cheese, egg and nutmeg and served in capon broth. (Wikipedia)

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Make dough
  2. Add cheese
  3. Cook in the oven
